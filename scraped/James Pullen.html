James Pullen

James is a long time bike enthusiast who cut his teeth on wrenching in the co-ops and community shops of Portland, OR and when not working on bikes he will occasionally ride them.  A native of the great state of Maine, James received his BS Ed. in Career/Technical & Technology Education along with Minor in Art Education from the University of Idaho in 2010.  His dream is to someday set up a High School Trade’s Program with a focus on Wood & Metal Work and Bicycle Mechanics.  It could happen.  His wife Jeneve and little dog Charlie are the center of his universe.
