Ryne Emory

Ryne is a global trekker and outdoor enthusiast.  Prior to working at the Bike Depot, she was a volunteer at the U.S. Peace Corps serving in Mali, West Africa where she worked on Sustainable Agriculture, Education and Gender Issues.  While in Africa, sleeping under the stars for two years, she developed a love for the outdoors and upon her return stateside moved to Colorado.  Ryne enjoys all types of bicycling- commuting, mountain biking and cruising around town.  When she's not bicycling, Ryne enjoys gardening, re-fabbing thrift store finds in one of her DIY projects and going to the mountains as often as possible.
