Fix Your Bike

Fix Your Bike is our flagship program where anyone can come to our shop during designated hours (see below) to utilize our space, tools and knowledge to fix their bikes.  It is a wonderful opportunity to meet your neighbors and others in the community that have a common interest in bicycles of all kinds.  The music is always bumping, the grease a-flying and bike passion burning!  We welcome everyone, regardless of bike clout, cool factor, gender, race, income, etc. 

We are Denver’s Community Bike Center!

You do not need prior bicycle knowledge, we have Volunteer Mechanics on hand to help with your maintenance and repair questions. The help of our volunteers is free, but all parts, both new and used, are sold separately.  As always, Bike Depot Members receive a discount on parts and accessories.  We have 6 benches, two wheel truing stations, a tube patching table and most any specialized tool you could want for wrenching bikes.

Volunteer mechanics are there to guide you and answer questions, but will not do the work for you!  If you would like us to service your bike, our staff mechanics would gladly do so for our standard service rates.

 

All participants must sign a waiver before their first visit and sign in every subsequent visit. 

There is no need to make an appointment, but space is limited and is first come, first serve.  Participants are not allowed to work on their bikes outside of designated Fix Your Bike Hours.
